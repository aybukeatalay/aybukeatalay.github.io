Aybuke Atalay

I am a PhD student at the School of Social and Political Science, University of Edinburgh. My research centers on computational propaganda, disinformation, and the influence of automation and algorithmic design on the consumption of political content. For my PhD thesis, I am investigating bots and bot-like behavior in online political discourse in Turkey. I co-organised the Summer Institute in Computational Social Science (SICSS-Edinburgh 2023) and served as the Research Coordinator of the Social Data Science Hub from 2023 to 2024.
